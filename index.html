Bio

I'm a front-end dev working for Berklee Online, an online school based at the famous Boston music college. I graduated summer 2015 from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, a 12-week full-time bootcamp designed to stuff as much practical web dev knowledge as can fit into its students' brains. I've been dabbling in HTML/CSS/Wordpress designs since I was in middle school, so I was delighted to finally dive into web development head-on.
